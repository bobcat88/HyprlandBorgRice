# Borg Brand Book: The Philosophy of Assimilation

## 1. Brand Identity: The Borg
The "Borg" brand is not about collective submission; it is about **Technological Perfection through Integration**. It takes the best of every ecosystem—Ilyamiro's fluidity, Material You's personalization, and CachyOS's raw performance—and assimilates them into a single, unified experience.

### Primary Asset: The Assimilated Arch
The official insignia is the **Assimilated Arch**—a synthesis of the Arch Linux silhouette and high-frequency Borg circuitry. It represents the union of a robust platform with advanced neural optimization.
![Assimilated Arch](file:///home/_johan/Documents/Projects/HyprlandConfig/shared/assets/assimilated-arch.svg)

### Brand Personality
- **Efficient**: No wasted pixels, no unnecessary overhead.
- **Adaptive**: Morphs between light/dark and various themes seamlessly.
- **Industrial-Minimalist**: Raw power hidden behind a clean, functional interface.
- **Fluid**: High-refresh-rate animations that feel like a biological extension of the user.

## 2. Visual Language

### Color Palette (Tactical & Cyber)
| Element | Hex Code | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Borg Deep** | `#0B0B0B` | Backgrounds, terminal surfaces |
| **Assimilation Green** | `#00FF41` | Accents, success states, terminal prompts |
| **Neural Cyan** | `#00F3FF` | Secondary accents, AI status, link hovers |
| **Bio-Grey** | `#1A1A1A` | Surface layers, inactive borders |
| **Warning Amber** | `#FFB000` | Alert states, critical updates |

### Typography
- **Monospace (Coding/Terminal)**: `JetBrains Mono` or `CaskaydiaCove Nerd Font`.
- **Interface (UI/Status Bars)**: `Inter` or `Outfit`.
- **Heading**: `Lexend` (for its geometric, modern feel).

### Style & Texture
- **Glassmorphism**: High-blur background behind transparent surfaces (neural link effect).
- **Industrial Gaps**: Strict 4px-6px gaps between windows to maintain visibility of the "core" (wallpaper).
- **Scanline Overlays**: Subtle 1% opacity scanline textures on the wallpaper or inactive windows.

---

## 3. Interaction Design (The "Borg Pulse")
Animations should be fast but "heavy." They shouldn't feel like toys; they should feel like mechanical components moving into place.
- **Windows**: Fast `popin` with a slight bounce (bezier curve).
- **Workspaces**: Smooth horizontal slides with high friction.
- **Special Workspace**: High-fade layer to signify entering the "Neural Link."
